# Ticket 001: Swing strategy backtest

We want a basic swing trading strategy that buys when the trend turns up
and sells when it turns down, using moving averages. It should size
positions based on how much we're willing to risk, and get out if the
trade goes against us too much. At the end we want to know how many
trades it made, how often it won, and what the overall profit and loss
looked like.
